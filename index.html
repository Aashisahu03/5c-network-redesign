How it works

From scan upload to signed report in four coordinated moves.

The platform handles intake, AI triage, radiologist assignment, and final delivery with a transparent workflow for every stakeholder.

01

Acquire and upload

DICOM studies enter through integrated PACS, centre dashboards, or secure upload channels.

02

AI triage and QC

Cases are prioritized, checked for quality, and enriched with modality-specific context.

03

Specialist routing

The right radiologist receives the right study based on subspecialty, SLA, and availability.

04

Report delivery

Structured reports return to the centre with timestamps, alerts, and operational analytics.
